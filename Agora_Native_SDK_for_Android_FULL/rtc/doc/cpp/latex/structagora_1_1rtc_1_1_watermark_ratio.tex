\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The position and size of the watermark on the screen.

The position and size of the watermark on the screen are determined by {\ttfamily x\+Ratio}, {\ttfamily y\+Ratio}, and {\ttfamily width\+Ratio}\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item ({\ttfamily x\+Ratio}, {\ttfamily y\+Ratio}) refers to the coordinates of the upper left corner of the watermark, which determines the distance from the upper left corner of the watermark to the upper left corner of the screen. The {\ttfamily width\+Ratio} determines the width of the watermark. 
\end{DoxyItemize}
