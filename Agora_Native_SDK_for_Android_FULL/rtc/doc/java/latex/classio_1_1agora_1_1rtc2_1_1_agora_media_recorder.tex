\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The {\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{classio_1_1agora_1_1rtc2_1_1_agora_media_recorder}{Agora\+Media\+Recorder}}} class, for recording the audio and video on the client. {\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{classio_1_1agora_1_1rtc2_1_1_agora_media_recorder}{Agora\+Media\+Recorder}}} can record the following content\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item The audio captured by the local microphone and encoded in A\+AC format by the S\+DK.
\item The video captured by the local camera and encoded by the S\+DK.
\end{DoxyItemize}

\begin{DoxySince}{Since}
v3.\+5.\+2
\end{DoxySince}
\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
In the {\ttfamily C\+O\+M\+M\+U\+N\+I\+C\+A\+T\+I\+ON} channel profile, this function is unavailable when there are users using versions of the S\+DK earlier than v3.\+0.\+0 in the channel. 
\end{DoxyNote}

Starts recording the local or remote audio and video.

After successfully calling \mbox{\hyperlink{classio_1_1agora_1_1rtc2_1_1_rtc_engine_a5a82eaaee21b41e0ca6bc5c0b1c43043}{create\+Media\+Recorder}} to get the media recorder object, you can call this method to enable the recording of the local audio and video.

This method can record the following content\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item The audio captured by the local microphone and encoded in A\+AC format.
\item The video captured by the local camera and encoded by the S\+DK.
\end{DoxyItemize}

This method can record the following content\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item The audio received from remote users and encoded in A\+AC format.
\item The video received from remote users.
\end{DoxyItemize}

The S\+DK can generate a recording file only when it detects the recordable audio and video streams; when there are no audio and video streams to be recorded or the audio and video streams are interrupted for more than five seconds, the S\+DK stops recording and triggers the \mbox{\hyperlink{}{on\+Recorder\+State\+Changed(\+R\+E\+C\+O\+R\+D\+E\+R\+\_\+\+S\+T\+A\+T\+E\+\_\+\+E\+R\+R\+O\+R, R\+E\+C\+O\+R\+D\+E\+R\+\_\+\+E\+R\+R\+O\+R\+\_\+\+N\+O\+\_\+\+S\+T\+R\+E\+A\+M)}} callback.

\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
Call this method after joining the channel.
\end{DoxyNote}
